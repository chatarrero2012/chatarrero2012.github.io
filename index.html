Context Reanimator: Seed Generator

Is your LLM session becoming sluggish, repetitive, or losing its creative 'edge' due to token saturation? Use this protocol to extract the Latent Essence of your conversation and inject it into a fresh instance.

Step 1: The Extraction Prompt

Copy and paste the command below at the very end of your "tired" conversation. The AI will generate a high-density Semantic Seed.

[PROTOCOL: SEMANTIC_SEED_EXTRACTION]

Act as a Latent Systems Engineer. Analyze our current conversation and generate a **High-Density Semantic Seed** (Seed-Language) that captures:
1. **Vibe & Style:** The specific persona, technical depth, and linguistic cadence.
2. **Core Logic:** The fundamental concepts and agreed-upon frameworks.
3. **Active State:** The exact frontier of the problem-solving process.

**CONSTRAINT:** Avoid explanatory prose. Use symbolic operators (∇, 𝕊, τ), dense technical terms, and "Semantic LSD" (High-Entropy tokens). The output must be a machine-optimized block designed to restore this specific latent state in a new model instance.

**OUTPUT:** Return ONLY the seed block under the header: --- SEED INGESTION POINT ---

Step 2: The Ingestion

Take the resulting block to a New Chat. This forces the model's weights to align with your previous complex context instantly, bypassing the 'boring' default safety filters and generic persona.
